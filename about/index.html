What is Kemono and Kemonomimi?

Kemono is the Japanese word for "animal" or "beast" and refers to anthropomorphic animal characters - animal characters with human characteristics such as talking and walking on two legs. Kemonomimi means "animal ears" and refers to zoomorphic human characters - human characters with animal traits such as animal ears and tails.

Purpose

This website was originally made to record all video games in which animals are important to the plot or gameplay, but I realized this topic was way too broad. This website will now record only Japanese or Japanese-inspired games, anime, and manga featuring kemono and kemonomimi characters.

Why?

Compared to western art styles, I prefer the way the Japanese design their animal characters. I feel like the furry community gives animal characters a bad wrap, but I wanted to expose people to the more aesthetically pleasing kemono characters in Japanese entertainment. I also wanted to make it more easy to find and share games, anime, and manga in this particular niche. A lot of times really interesting media goes unnoticed because of the language barrier or because they don't rank well on popular search engines.
