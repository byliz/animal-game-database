Here's a makeshift little "forum" comment area space-thing where you can leave comments on what you think about the site and any entry suggestions you have!


A few things to keep in mind when leaving a comment on this website:


1. Please be respectful.
2. Before making suggestions please check if it's already been added by using the search bar in the navigation panel or entering a URL in your browser's address bar in the following format: https://agdb.smugcroc.com/#(gametitle). Replace (gametitle) with the title of the game in all lower case and with no spaces.
3. To make a suggestion, tell me the title of the game, your name, and your Twitter or website URL to credit you as a suggester on the game entry!
