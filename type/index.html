Filter by Type

There are two "types" on this list: kemono and kemonomimi. Kemono is the Japanese word for "animal" or "beast" and refers to anthropomorphic animal characters - animal characters with human characteristics such as talking and walking on two legs. Kemonomimi means "animal ears" and refers to zoomorphic human characters - human characters with animal traits such as animal ears and tails.
